My Research Interest

My current research topics concern large-scale system coordination and machine learning algorithm in distributed systems. In my research group, we study a technique called Aggregate Computing, a top-down declarative approach to coordinate collective behaviour. Over the years, my colleagues developed novel tool-chain to use this new paradigm, such as ScaFi and Protelis.

My current trend consists in merging Aggregate Computing with Machine learning, gaining benefits of both worlds.

I am also the creator of ScaFi-Web, a web-based tool to use Aggregate Computing directly on the web.
